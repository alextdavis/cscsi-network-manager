My Home Network


Set an administrator password: This aditional password is different from your network password. This administrator password will allow you to login to My Home Network to check in on the on your network and allow you to:

  • Interact with an intuitive visualization of your home network setup
  • Manage settings (network name & password, security preferences, etc)
  • Control who is on your network and what they can access
  • View home network performance statistics
